Why You Should Attend SRA

If you are looking to work in to your chosen profession you will need to harness quality education in order ton acquire the knowledge and skills needed. Our goal at SatireRecords Academy is to teach our students how to explore the world of sound and transform their passion for music and audio into a dream career while in the process, create music producers & sound engineers who are well prepared for the competitive industry challenges that lie ahead.


Experienced Instructors

Our instructors are dedicated to learning about your personal musical goals and helping you achieve them. All of our instructors have either conservatory or music school training and most have professional performance experience. We hire instructors that are warm and approachable and are dedicated to helping you develop the style of music that you want to learn.




Recording Studio

Satire Records Academy has a fully functional recording studio and this gives us an edge in being able to expose students to real-world view of production and engineering session life during and after their training.




Location

Our school is conveniently located in a quiet residential area at 116a Adetola Street, Aguda-Surulere, Lagos.
