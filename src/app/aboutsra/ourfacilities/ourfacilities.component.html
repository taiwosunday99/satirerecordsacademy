Our Facilities

Media Ground

Our Media Hub, a 10 Seat classroom, contains 5 Intel Desktop Computers and are loaded with Cubase, Reason, FL Studio, Slate Digital, Waves, and other creative audio production software. There is also extra space that can accomodate 5 Student Laptops. We limited the number of seats available to better our teacher to student ratio and to ensure there's enough one on one interaction in all classes.

Production Studio

Our production studio is also equipped with an Intel Mac Mini running the DAWs; Cubase, Reason, FL Studio 12, Logic and also the Waves, Slate Digital, Native Instruments, Brainworx and many more plugins. The Studio also has a Vocal booth for recording vocals and instruments. All students have access to our production studio during their training.
