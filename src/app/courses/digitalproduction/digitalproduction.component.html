CERTIFICATION COURSE

Digital Music Production

The aim of this Short Course in Music Production is to enlighten students to the processes of a high quality music production.The information and content of this course will be delivered by hands on training and by practicing “real-world”scenarios. Recording, editing and mixing techniques will be taught based on Logic Pro / FL Studio / Cubase.

Area of Focus

Our course modules consist of practical and theory components of the course, covering at least one complete subject area. Each module progressively builds the student’s skills toward the completion of educational and technical skills goals for the program. To give you professional insight, students will also take part in production sessions involving real artists and supervised by the instructors.
