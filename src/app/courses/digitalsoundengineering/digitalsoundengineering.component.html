Digital Sound Engineering

The Satire Records Digital Sound Engineering Course (DSE) is a non-degree program designed to get applying students into the audio engineering field in just 3 months with focus on the foundations of sound engineering.

The course is intensive practically and theoretically to prepare students to become working success stories in the commercial sound industry. Students taking this course have the opportunity to learn both the technical and creative components of audio recording.

Area of Focus

Our course modules consist of practical and theory components of the course, covering at least one complete subject area. Each module progressively builds the student’s skills toward the completion of educational and technical skills goals for the program. To give you professional insight, students will also take part in production sessions involving real artists and supervised by the instructors.
